Each student learns in a different way and has a unique set of capacities and needs. We use adaptive techniques to address the individual needs of students. Our system diagnosis students within a certain curriculum and dynamically builds a unique learning path for every student.
Adaptive quizzes can be built for your custom curriculum. You can monitor the progress of students, and adjust the adaptability rules (e.g., which learning resource to use based on the response of an assessment item).
Students can independently take tests to check their level of understanding and parents can access this information to help address important issues.
