Privacy Policy

This section explains our policy regarding the collection use and disclosure of information obtained by us. Please understand that by submitting any Personally Identifiable Information to us, you consent and agree that we may collect, use and disclose such Personally Identifiable Information in accordance with this Privacy Policy and our Terms of Use, and as permitted or required by law. If you do not agree with these terms, then please do not provide any Personally Identifiable Information to us. This policy may change so please refer back to it periodically.
Collection and Use of Information
When you create an account on our Site, we ask you to supply us with personal information so that we can communicate with you regarding your account. With your permission we also use your e-mail address to send you newsletters, surveys and similar communications. We keep track of your interactions with us, including but not limited to your site activity. We use this information for such purposes as enhancing our service offering for our customers.
Age Restrictions
You must be 18 years of age or older to use our free services in this Site. For school accounts, individuals under the age of 13 may utilize the service, they may do so only with the involvement of a responsible adult who agrees to our Terms of Use. While we do have content that may be watched by children, we do not intentionally collect information from individuals 13 years of age and younger.
Cookies
When you log in to your Icon Marh account, cookies are placed on your computer's hard drive. (Cookies are small pieces of data that are sent to your browser from a Web server and stored on your hard drive for record keeping purposes.) The cookies will store enough information to automatically log you in to your account the next time you visit the Site. If your browser is set to not accept cookies, you will not be able to use this feature.
Confidentiality & Security of Personally Identifiable Information
We consider the confidentiality and security of your information to be of the utmost importance. We will not sell, rent or disclose your personal information to third parties. We will use commercially reasonable efforts to keep your personally identifiable information private and will not share it with third parties, except as otherwise provided in this Privacy Policy. Please note that we do not guarantee the security of personally identifiable information, and there is some risk that an unauthorized third party may find a way to circumvent our security systems or that transmission of your information over the Internet will be intercepted. In the event that Icon Math is acquired by or merged with a third party, we reserve the right to sell or otherwise transfer the personal information we have collected as part of that transaction. If the third party has a different privacy policy than ours, we will notify you in advance and give you an opportunity to prevent your personal information from being shared.
